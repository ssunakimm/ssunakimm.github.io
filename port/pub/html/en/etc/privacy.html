Privacy Policy

Welcome to the website of SAMSUNG C&T JAPAN.
SAMSUNG C&T JAPAN engages in the highest efforts to protect personal information provided by its customers in compliance with the Act on the
Protection of Personal Information, and other applicable laws and guidelines.

  • Collection of Personal Information

    The Company may collect personal information from customers within the scope required for providing better customer service. The Company collects personal information from customers for limited purposes, and relies on legitimate and fair means in collecting personal information.

  • Items of Personal Information Collected and Used

    The Company collects the e-mail addresses of customers in order to respond to customer suggestions and inquiries.

  • Use of Personal Information

    The Company uses the collected personal information solely for the purpose of responding to customer inquiries.

  • Disclosure of Personal Information

    Personal information provided by a customer will not be disclosed by the Company to any third party without the customer’s consent, except as required by law.

  • Presentation and Update of Personal Information

    The Company responds within a reasonable period of time to customers’ requests for renewal, update and deletion of the personal information they have provided.

  • Amendment of the Policy

    The Company may change the Privacy Policy if applicable laws are revised. The Company will continuously improve the Policy.

  • Partnership

    The Company’s partner for user visit analysis, Google, logs and analyzes user visits to this website. The Company has entered into a confidentiality agreement with the partner in relation to the handling of personal information.

    For the partner’s privacy policy, please refer to “How Google uses data when you use our partners’ sites or apps” (http://www.google.com/intl/en/policies/privacy/partners/).

  • Management of Personal Information

    In order to ensure proper management of personal information, the Company appoints and supervises privacy managers and provides employees with relevant training.

    The Company also applies reasonable safety measures including blocking of illegitimate access to personal information and prevention of loss, damage or leakage of personal information.

  • Protection of Personal Information on Linked Websites

    The Company is not responsible for the handling of personal information on the linked websites of businesses or individuals other than the website of the Company.
