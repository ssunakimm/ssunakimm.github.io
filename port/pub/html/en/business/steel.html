Steel

Our steel business is active in many regions, spanning more than 30 countries and diverse fields, based on more than 50 years of steel trading experience, accumulated know-how, and industry knowledge. The steel products we trade are used for a range of applications, from big items such as ships, bridges, buildings, and tanks to televisions, kitchenware, and pipes.

Export
We supply Japanese steel worldwide, mainly in Korea. Japanese steel is used in many industries, including shipbuilding, energy, infrastructure, automobiles, and home appliances, which has contributed to the long-term success of Japanese steel manufacturers.
Import
