Materials

Our materials business provides the Samsung Group and its partners with high-quality, high-performance, technologically superior Japanese electronic components and materials. The materials we supply become part of Samsung's advanced products, including smartphones and televisions, helping enrich our customers' lives. In addition, we are engaged in a wide range of businesses, including with global giants outside of the Samsung Group, re-marketing/recycling business in collaboration with the headquarters in Korea, and localization of semiconductor materials.

Contact Us
Sourcing/Support Business
We provide a sourcing service to supply electronic components and materials from Japanese companies to the Samsung Group's production plants. We also offer purchasing agency services for foreign companies that lack a purchasing base in Japan, and provide introduction and evaluation support for next-generation technologies and materials for Samsung Group.
Re-Marketing/Recycle Business
We recycle idle assets and resources, such as through buying and selling used semiconductor devices and recovering and refining battery scrap.
Semiconductor Material Localization Business
Based on Samsung Electronics' needs, we attract materials manufacturers' factories near Samsung's semiconductor plant and support their entry into the global market.
Item
  • Display material

    We sell optical films, adhesives, additives and other chemical products used in polarizers, which are key components of TVs and IT products (Note PC, Monitors, Tablet, Mobile), and raw materials for color resists for CMOS image sensors to Samsung Group companies.

  • Semiconductor material

    We sell front-end related materials to major Korean semiconductor manufacturers such as Samsung Electronics and SK Hynix, and semiconductor related materials such as Epoxy Molding Compound (EMC) silica for back-end packaging processes and electro-deposited copper foil for smartphone substrates.

  • Printer and MFP Materials

    We sell toner products (polymerization and pulverization), various raw materials necessary for toner production, sensor parts, and other parts and materials used in printers and MFPs to overseas manufacturers.

  • Recycled Metal Materials

    Import and sales in Japan of nickel, cobalt, and lithium-related products derived from recycled battery materials refined in Korea. We are also engaged in the import/export business of various metal products including rare earths such as neodymium.

  • Development of Process Materials

    We are engaged in product marketing for various businesses of Samsung Group's affiliated companies, from finding and introducing high-functional materials and next-generation materials in Japan to establishing supply chains.

  • Used Semiconductor Devices

    We sell used semiconductor manufacturing equipment from the factories of semiconductor manufacturers in Japan at prices based on domestic and international demand. Additionally, we market used equipment we and our partners currently have for the domestic demand price.
