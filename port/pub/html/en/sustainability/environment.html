Environment

 
Minimizing Environmental Impact
through Eco-friendly Management
 

The effects of corporate activities created in the process of rapid economic growth, such as insensitive use of fossil fuels and damage to the natural environment, have contributed to climate change and global warming. The international community has set common goals and enforced environmental regulations to solve these environmental problems while global companies are taking the initiative in protecting the global environment as they share the need to take corporate responsibility for the environment.

In a measure to respond to today’s calls to action and address these environmental problems, Samsung C&T takes careful consideration of the environment aspects in its business operation and strives to minimize environmental impact through improved environmental management in the production of products and services, and utilization of environmentally friendly products and services.
