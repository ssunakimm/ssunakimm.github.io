Application and recruiting process
Application requirements
(New graduates)
  • Position

    Fresh Graduates Recruitment

  • Applicant eligibility

    Candidates of any nationality, who have graduated from a 4-year university or postgraduate course, or expect to have graduated, with no previous career experience

  • Salary

    Initial salary (As of April, 2023）245,000 Japanese Yen for bachelor’s or master’s degree holder

  • Allowances

    Commuting expenses, overtime allowance, housing allowance, Meal allowance

  • Pay raise

    Once a year（March)

  • Bonus payment

    Twice a year（June & December)

  • Work location

    Tokyo

  • Working hours

    8:30～17:00 (We offer staggered working hours.)

  • Days off, holidays

    Full five-day workweek（Days off: Sat & Sun, national holidays, New Year’s holiday, yearly paid holiday, special leave（family events, etc.）)

  • Insurance

    Health insurance, welfare pension insurance, unemployment insurance, workers' compensation insurance

  • Welfare

    Retirement plan, welfare service program

  • Training
    • Specific training（New hire training, new promotee training)
    • Selected training（Working in Seoul HQ, Regional Specialist Program, business school programs）
    • Language education（Korean, English）, online education for learning at home, various professional training
