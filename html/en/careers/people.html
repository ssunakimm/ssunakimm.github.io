Work
What's your responsibility in your department?
As the head of the chemistry group, I am responsible for developing the department's strategy, mentoring department members, and managing performance.
Additionally, I am involved in developing new agendas that will become drivers for future growth.
What was your motivation for applying?
Also, what made you decide to join us?
I became interested in Korea through exchanges with the sister school during my school days, so I applied to Samsung C&T Japan because I wanted to become a bridge between Korea and Japan.
I think that the response of the seniors who interviewed me at the time was very kind and that I felt that it was a company that valued people, and that’s what motivated me to decide to join this company.
What do you consider most important in your work?
Sharing information is important. There is a limit to what one can do alone, and I believe that having all internal and external stakeholders aware of the same information and situation leads to early decision-making.
It's true that bad news is difficult to deliver, but I think we can find a better solution by delivering it quickly.
Please tell us about the assignment you think you have been the most successful.
While anticipating mid- to long-term increase market demand and visiting the country several times with my business partners, I was able to feel a sense of accomplishment as well as reward when I saw that the deals I had continued to conduct market research and sales activities for were actually growing significantly.
Life
How do you spend your time after work or on holidays?
I have a long commute to work and only spend a short time at home on weekdays, so on the weekends I go to the park with my kids, eat out, and spend as much time as possible with my family.
How do you spend your time after work or on holidays?
My hobby is playing golf. I started playing golf after receiving a club from a senior employee. Although my golf skills are not improving, I think it has become a good hobby that is also good for business.
Do you clearly separate business and personal matters?
During holidays, the company rarely contacts me regarding work. Also, I think I have a good work-life balance because I can take vacations relatively freely according to my family's schedule.

Work
What's your responsibility in your department?
I am a manager of in Support Group and am responsible for human resources and secretary to the president.
What do you find rewarding about your work?
I believe that the most rewarding and attractive aspect of human resources is being able to relate deeply to people in a variety of situations.
What this means is that I can contribute to the company's organizational issues such as recruiting excellent talent, shaping people's careers and motivation, staffing, organizational culture, and performance evaluations.
What do you consider most important in your work?
I think the most important thing is relationships with people. My goal is to listen carefully to each and every employee's voice and improve Samsung C&T Japan as a whole into a better working environment for everyone, so I think communication with people is very important.
Please tell us about the most challenging assignment
you've accomplished.
During the period when face-to-face communication between employees decreased due to COVID-19 and there was almost no interaction, we planned an online exchange meeting to prevent employees from becoming isolated, but online meetings clearly had limits, so I looked for ways to promote in-house exchange among employees.
Fortunately, the pandemic has come to an end, and we will be able to host company-wide events and volunteer work starting in 2023.
Life
How do you spend your time after work or on holidays?
After work, I pick up my child from daycare, make dinner, and take care of my child. Our family's routine is to eat out every weekend for breakfast and then go to the park. Since I cannot play with my child on weekdays, I try to spend as much time with my child as possible on the weekends.
How do you spend your time after work or on holidays?
I'm always looking for 'fun things' to do with my child, including games and toy-making that parents and children can do together.
Do you clearly separate business and personal matters?
I use staggered working hours, so I start at 7:00 in the morning and leave work at 16:00. But I can adjust my working hours flexibly.

Work
What's your responsibility in your department?
I am responsible for buying and selling used semiconductor manufacturing equipment. There are numerous semiconductor factories in Japan. In addition to purchasing used equipment for semiconductor manufacturing that has been used in such factories by bidding and selling them to Japanese and foreign customers, I also introduce and sell used equipment Samsung C&T Japan can introduce to customers or used equipment in other countries to Japanese customers.
What was your motivation for applying?
Also, what made you decide to join us?
I applied to Samsung C&T Japan because I thought I could connect Japan and the world, enrich people's lives, and realize better things by leveraging the networks the company has all over the world.
The reason why I decided to join Samsung C&T Japan was not only because I thought I could realize the motivation I just mentioned for applying but also because, in the case of our company, which is headquartered in Korea, there was an opportunity to use the Korean language I studied in university.
What do you consider most important in your work?
I always keep in mind that I have to think from the other person's perspective. During discussions with customers, there are times when we insist on different conditions or views.
In such cases, I first consider what the customer is concerned about and what they expect from us to make efforts to facilitate smooth negotiations.
Please tell us about the most memorable episode from
your interactions with customers.
When I was having a meal with a supplier of a certain product, I heard about a new product that his company was working on, which gave me an opportunity to discuss a new product. It was an opportunity to realize that there are business opportunities in communication outside of formal meeting places.
Life
How do you spend your time after work or on holidays?
I often relax by watching dramas or entertainment programs on TV or Netflix.
How do you spend your time after work or on holidays?
To recharge myself, I still occasionally play baseball on holidays,
which I used to do during my school days. My best position is second base.
Do you clearly separate business and personal matters?
I believe that vague boundaries are bad for both business and personal life,
so I always remind myself to control my pace.

Work
What's your responsibility in your department?
I have two missions:
① to expand sales of biomass fuel and ② to become a bridge between Japan and Korea.
① Increasing sales and market share by expanding sales of biomass fuel to power plants in Japan
② Explore Japan’s trends regarding biomass fuel, collaborate with energy companies, and carry out projects with headquarters in Korea.
What do you consider most important in your work?
It is about having an opinion and adequately explaining the reasons. Although this is fundamental, I think it is important to be ready to clearly explain your opinion. When meeting with outsiders, I try to fulfill my responsibilities as a representative of the Samsung Group.
Please tell us about the assignment you think you have been the most successful.
My happiest memory was when I received my first order from a customer with whom I had been negotiating a transaction for three years at the department level. I learned the importance of always keeping in mind that business opportunities could always happen through close communication with customers.
How do you want to grow in this company?
In the future, I would like to work on developing new businesses. Given that our company is the Japanese entity of Samsung C&T, I believe that our big mission is to create businesses with Japan as the axis.
We would like to help our company take the lead in businesses related to industries of the future, not limited to biomass, and further contribute to society.

After playing soccer or working out at the gym,
