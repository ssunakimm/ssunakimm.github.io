Corporate-level compliance programs
for settling a culture of integrity

In response to society’s demand for stronger corporate compliance monitoring
systems as well as the company’s own need to strengthen management
transparency, the company carries out a wide range of compliance
activities including those for establishing a culture of integrity,
creating autonomous compliance organizations at business departments,
and compliance-related training/audit/evaluation/reward programs.
