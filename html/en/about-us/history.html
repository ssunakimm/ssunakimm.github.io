History

 

Samsung C&T Japan, the first overseas office for Samsung Group,
began its operations in Japan in 1953 and has served as a bridge between Japan and South Korea since then.
With over 70 years of experience, Samsung C&T Japan aims to strengthen its business role and capability,
and stand at the center of global business.
