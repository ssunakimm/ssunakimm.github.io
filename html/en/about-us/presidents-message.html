CEO Message

 
Samsung C&T Japan Corporation
President & C.E.O Wonseok Jung

We sincerely thank you for your continued support.

Our business was founded in Japan in 1953, and has been operating now for more than 70 years.

In those 70 years, we have grown as a company while successfully responding to customer needs and developing new products under Samsung's management philosophy of "leveraging human resources and technologies, creating the best products and services, and contributing to society."

In the current business environment, the outlook continues to be uncertain. An uncertain economic situation and instability in the financial markets are being caused by prolonged geopolitical risks, the weakening Yen, and the worsening impact rising prices are having on consumption.

Even in this challenging and ever-changing environment, we aim to pursue sustainable growth by actively taking on challenges and expanding our business, with a focus on chemicals, steel, materials, and energy.

Samsung C&T Japan's executives and employees will work toward achieving these goals, and tackle challenges as a unified team.

We ask for your continued support and encouragement.
